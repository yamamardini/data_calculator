Amimal products

Meat products are a primary source of high-quality proteins, essential for building and maintaining muscle tissues in the body.

In addition to protein, meat contains a variety of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin B12, iron, and zinc, which contribute to vital body functions such as energy production and immune support.

The nutritional value varies depending on the type of meat and its cuts, such as beef, lamb, chicken, and fish. Consuming meat in a moderate and balanced manner can be part of a healthy diet.
