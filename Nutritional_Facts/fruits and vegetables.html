Nutritional Facts

furits and vegetables

Fruits and vegetables are essential components of a healthy diet, providing a wide range of vitamins,
minerals, and dietary fiber necessary for maintaining overall health. These natural foods contain

antioxidants that help protect cells from damage and boost the immune system.
Incorporating a variety of fruits and vegetables into daily meals helps reduce the
risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and diabetes.

It is preferable to consume fresh and seasonal fruits and vegetables to obtain the highest nutritional value.
In the following sections, you will find detailed information about different types of fruits and vegetables,
their nutritional values, and health benefits, as outlined in the tables below.
