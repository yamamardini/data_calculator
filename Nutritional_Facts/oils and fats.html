Nutritional Facts

oils and fats

Oils and fats play a crucial role in the diet, providing concentrated energy and
aiding in the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins such as vitamins A, D, E, and K.

Fats come in several forms, including saturated, unsaturated, and trans fats,
each having different effects on health.

Unsaturated fats, such as those found in plant oils,
are beneficial for heart health when consumed in moderation.

However, it is important to limit the intake of saturated and trans fats, which may
be associated with an increased risk of cardiovascular diseases.
